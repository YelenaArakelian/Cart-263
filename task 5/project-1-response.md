# Group 4 - Museum Ticket

I found this project really interesting for the message on culture appropriation. Not only does the project look aesthetically pleasing, but it proves a strong point.

The game begins with a login page where the player is asked to enter an email and answear a question: " Are you a person of color?" Depending on the response, the ticket price changes: 15$ for "yes" and 10$ for "no". This immediatly introduces a stattement on inequality and encourages players to reflect on how identity can affect privileges, access and experiences.

One of the visual elements that stood out was the use of silouhettes wearing traditional clothing. The silhouettes have a strong presence and contribute to the overall meaning of the project. Once the user hovers over them, the silouhettes become blurry, which creates an interesting visual effect and interactive layer to the user's experience. This small interaction encourages the player to engage with elements on the screen while reinforcing the critical thinking on idenity and representation can sometimes become misunderstood and distorted.
